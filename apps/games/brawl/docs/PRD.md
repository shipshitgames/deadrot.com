# Brawl — TinyPRD

- **Genre:** one-on-one battlefield fighting game.
- **Core loop:** pick a faction fighter, face a game-selected rival, win the round, rematch.
- **Win / Lose:** win = opponent health reaches 0; lose = player health reaches 0 or timer ends behind on health.
- **Controls:** horizontal movement, jump, guard, light attack, heavy attack, special attack.
- **Mode:** solo arcade duel.
- **Scope cut list:** no online PvP, no campaign ladder, no final sprite sheets, no full roster balance.
- **Juice budget:** hit sparks, guard sparks, camera shake, KO banner, health bars, battlefield depth.
