# Brawl — TinyPRD

- **Genre:** battlefield fighting game — 1v1 Duel and Smash-style Arena.
- **Core loop:** pick a faction fighter, pick a mode, win the match, rematch.
  - **Duel:** face a game-selected rival; win the round on health.
  - **Arena:** 2-4 fighter free-for-all (you plus AI fill) on a platform stage with side ledges.
- **Win / Lose:**
  - **Duel:** win = opponent health reaches 0; lose = player health reaches 0 or timer ends behind on health.
  - **Arena:** damage-percent knockback + ring-outs cost stocks (3 each); last fighter standing wins, or most stocks (then least damage) when the timer ends.
- **Controls:** horizontal movement, jump (double-jump for air recovery in Arena), guard, light/heavy/special attack.
- **Modes:** solo arcade duel, local/AI arena brawl.
- **Scope cut list:** no online PvP, no campaign ladder, no final sprite sheets, no full roster balance, no stage hazards/pickups yet.
- **Juice budget:** hit sparks, guard sparks, camera shake, KO/ring-out feedback, health bars, arena damage+stock scoreboard, battlefield depth, dynamic arena camera.
