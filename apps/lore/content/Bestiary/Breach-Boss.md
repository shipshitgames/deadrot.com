# Breach-Boss

**At a glance:** [[Scourge]] breach boss · massive non-human war body grown around a toxic-green chest breach heart · holds a region with melee pressure and ranged barrages, shields its core and enrages when wounded · appears in [[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Deadlane]] / [[Rothulk]].

Monstrous Scourge expression grown around a breach heart.

## Behaviour
- Holds a major breach or region.
- Uses melee pressure plus ranged barrages.
- Can shield around its core and enrage when wounded.

## Gameplay Read
- Massive non-human body.
- Chest breach core.
- Shield-organ ring and projectile vents.

## Visual Motifs
- Toxic-green breach heart.
- Wet chitin fused with rusted metal.
- Horn or mandible crown.
- Asymmetry and parasite growth, not a larger soldier.
