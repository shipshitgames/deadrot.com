# Render

**At a glance:** [[Scourge-Host-Families|Chitin Warhost]] elite · melee charger, no ranged attack · straight-line charge, no flanking · enrages at half health (speed + ferocity spike) · appears in [[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Deadlane]].

A Render is what the [[Scourge]] makes when it finds a soldier. The
[[Scourge-Host-Families|Chitin Warhost]] were a shell-world warrior race once — big, plated,
built to fight — and the parasite kept all of that and threw away the parts that hesitate. It
cracked the carapace, threaded itself through the gaps, and pointed the result at the nearest
thing worth breaking.

A [[The-Wardens|Warden]] gunner gets one clean look before it reaches the wall: low, wide,
moving faster than that much shell has any right to, green light bleeding from the seams of a
carapace that used to belong to something with a name. Nobody calls it in by that name. On
the line they just call it the part of the wave that gets there first.

## Behaviour
- Charges the nearest fortified position or high-value target in a straight line — no flanking, no hesitation.
- Armored leading carapace absorbs the opening volley; breach nodes beneath the seams glow as a health cue.
- At half health the parasite purges restraint organelles: speed and ferocity spike hard (enrage). Nodes burst open, exposing raw tissue.
- No ranged attack. Pure melee pressure. Forces repositioning in any lane or breach room where standing still means dying.

## Gameplay Read
- Wide, hunched silhouette — lower than expected, which makes it hard to pick out of a crowd.
- Toxic-green breach nodes visible at carapace seams; rupture signals enrage.
- Enrage: nodes split open, exposed tissue shifts blood-hot red; charge speed increases visibly.

## Visual Motifs
- Thick alien carapace, black and iridescent, cracked by parasite growth seeping through the seams.
- Blade-claw forelimbs — elongated, bone-pale, serrated at the inner edge.
- Parasite tissue bulging through shoulder joints and beneath the faceplate.
- No visible eyes — sensory organs replaced by parasite nodes; the breach drives it.
