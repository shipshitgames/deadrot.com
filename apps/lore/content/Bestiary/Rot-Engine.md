# Rot-Engine

**At a glance:** [[Scourge-Host-Families|Machine-Graft]] [[Scourge]] elite siege platform · plants at range and dissolves the lane with a corrosive bile lance · swollen front bile sac is both the threat and the weak point · appears in [[Deadlane]] / [[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Redline]].

A [[Scourge-Host-Families|Machine-Graft]] [[Scourge]] elite built from a conquered heavy vehicle — transport, hauler, or field-artillery chassis — gutted and reborn as a bile-artillery platform. It never needs to close in. It plants at range and dissolves the lane.

## Behaviour
- Stops at distance and holds position; unlike [[Graft-Breacher]] it does not advance into melee.
- Primary attack: sustained bile lance from a toxic-green sac fused into the former engine cavity — a pressurised stream of corrosive fluid that eats through tower armour and fortified walls over seconds of dwell time.
- The lance is a structure-killer and zone-denier, not an infantry weapon; it cannot track fast-moving targets.
- Secondary: when pushed into close range or flanked, the sac vents radially — bile burst that injures clustered infantry and coats the ground in toxic slick, slowing any advance.
- Acts as fire support in mixed waves: the swarm rushes while the Rot-Engine pins defenders behind corroding cover that is already buying the rippers time.

## Gameplay Read
- Low, wide vehicle profile — ground-hugging, tracked or multi-wheeled chassis; reads distinctly from [[Graft-Breacher]]'s upright walker frame.
- The bile sac dominates the front mass: it is the threat and the obvious weak point simultaneously.
- Counter-play has two correct answers, each with a cost — *shoot the sac* at range to force a detonation that splashes bile onto nearby [[Swarm-Ripper|Rippers]] and [[Swarm-Spitter|Spitters]]; or *advance past its minimum range* to deny the lance entirely (closing into the blast radius of the vent).
- High-value variant: the chassis sometimes carries a [[CANON|Choir relay node]], extending the Choir's reach into the lane. Destroying this variant frenzies severed units nearby for a short window. Flagged by a second active toxic-green node on the hull.

## Visual Motifs
- Former vehicle chassis — low, wide, track- or wheel-mounted; machine origin still legible beneath the infestation.
- Cockpit hollow and breached: no operator, no glass — fibrous wet tissue packed into the cab.
- Bile sac swollen past the burst bodywork of the engine bay, held in place by ribs of split chassis metal.
- Black chitin crawling up the hull panels from axle to roofline, heavier on one side.
- Organic ducts where fuel lines were — thick, pulsing under pressure, bile-green through semi-transparent tissue walls.
- Old vehicle markings — load ratings, caution stripes, fleet numbers — barely legible under the growth.

## Sprite Brief
- Views: front, side, back.
- Must read as wheeled or tracked vehicle, not a biped — wide, low, close to the ground. Clearly distinct from [[Graft-Breacher]].
- Front: bile sac is the dominant mass; hollow cockpit sits behind it; threat and weak point both immediately obvious.
- Side: low ground clearance; organic ducting along the chassis length; sac pressure visible.
- Back: least consumed — intact hull plate, the safest approach angle.
- Scale: similar footprint to [[Graft-Breacher]], significantly lower.
