# Scourge Host Families

**At a glance:** [[Scourge]] bestiary taxonomy · one parasite grammar (green breach cores, black chitin, overwritten host) worn over six conquered-host families: flesh, chitin, mycelial, machine-graft, bone titan, voidship · every batch records threat role + host family · spans [[Deadlane]] / [[Starblight]] / [[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Pactfall]].

The Scourge is one parasite wearing many conquered worlds.

Every Scourge unit should share a default parasite grammar, but the host underneath can
come from different species, machines, colonies, or craft. This gives the army variety
without breaking faction cohesion.

## Default Scourge Grammar

These traits should appear across the army:

- Toxic-green breach cores or parasite nodes.
- Black chitin, wet raw tissue, exposed sinew, tendrils, and rupture seams.
- Host material being overwritten: flesh, bone, armor, metal, fungus, hull plating, stone,
  or unknown alien anatomy.
- Asymmetry and invasive growth, not clean creature design.
- Weapons grown out of host anatomy: claws, bone blades, throat sacs, arm lances, spore
  vents, projectile organs, shield organs.

If a design lacks this grammar, it is not Scourge yet; it is only a monster.

## Host Families

### Rot-Infested Flesh Hosts

The default first-read Scourge form. Humanoid or animal bodies split open by parasite
growth, with ruined armor or bone still visible.

Use for: base swarm, early Scourge Survivors enemies, common Deadlane waves.

### Chitin Warhosts

Conquered shell-world or insectoid species. Hard carapace, hooked limbs, mandibles, claw
clusters, and blade growths under parasite control.

Use for: fast melee variants, elite chargers, armored swarm replacements.

### Mycelial Spore Hosts

Conquered fungal colonies and spore organisms. Bulging sacs, breathing vents, fibrous
growth, root-like tendrils, and clouds implied by silhouette.

Use for: spitters, area denial units, Starblight mines, breach growth.

### Machine-Graft Hosts

Industrial drones, vehicles, weapons, and battlefield machines hijacked from conquered
worlds. Metal is not clean tech anymore; it is a shell the parasite grows through.

Use for: ranged units, tower-breakers, Deadlane siege enemies, infected weapons.

### Bone Titan Hosts

Large alien megafauna or warrior species stripped down to bone plates, horns, rib cages,
and massive limb frames, then rebuilt by parasite tissue.

Use for: bosses, minibosses, Pactfall neutral objectives, heavy Deadlane waves.

### Voidship Hosts

Ships, drones, orbital wreckage, and vacuum-adapted bodies conquered before reaching the
surface war. Hull plates become armor; engines become organs; cables become tendrils.

Use for: [[Starblight]], [[Scourge-Fighter]], [[Orbital-Breach-Carrier]], orbital bosses.

## Batch Rule

For any Scourge generation batch, record both:

- **Threat role:** Ripper, Spitter, elite, boss, craft, carrier, etc.
- **Host family:** flesh, chitin, mycelial, machine-graft, bone titan, voidship, or a new
  conquered-world family.

The same threat role can have multiple host-family variants. A Ripper can be a flesh host
in Scourge Survivors, a chitin warhost in Deadlane, and a voidship parasite in Starblight.
