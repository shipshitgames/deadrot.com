# The Scourge

**At a glance:** through-line antagonist faction/affliction · host-dependent parasite with no native form, wears what it infects · hive mind over [[CANON|the Choir]] with limited radius — sever the relays and it goes feral · appears in [[Deadlane]] / [[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Starblight]].

The endless, mutating horde that pours from the [[Cosmology|breaches]] — the through-line
antagonist of the entire universe. Every Ship Shit Games title is, at its core, a
**Scourge** game. Closest analogue: the **Zerg** — a swarming, adapting hive-thing with
**no single general**.

## Nature
A spreading intelligence, not a commander. It overwhelms through mass and mutation.
The Scourge is best understood as a **host-dependent parasite** (see [[CANON]] §1): it wears
what it infects, has **no native form**, and is **not evil** — only driven to survive and
spread, an AI-like mind with no off-switch. It consumes hosts to death and so always needs
more; it has crossed and consumed worlds before this one (and **jumps on** when one is spent),
so the army is full of different host races and ruined technologies wearing the same parasite
grammar.

## The Choir & its weakness
The Scourge is a **hive mind** — it knows everything its parts know at once, over a signal
called **the Choir** ([[CANON]] §5). There is no general to kill; the mind *is* the
connection. But the Choir has a **limited radius**, forcing the swarm to mass densely and to
plant **repeaters/relays** to extend reach — and those can be destroyed. Sever a node (kill
its repeaters, or push it past the radius) and the Scourge goes **feral**: still lethal, but
blind, dumb, instinct-only. **Isolation** is how every game fights it ([[CANON]] §6).

That keeps the bestiary open for demonic silhouettes, alien organs, fungal colonies,
machine grafts, corrupted ships, and later regional expressions without breaking canon.
Use [[Scourge-Host-Families]] to keep those forms coherent.

## Visual identity
The Scourge has one default design language across many hosts: toxic-green breach cores,
black chitin, wet tissue, tendrils, rupture seams, and host material being consumed.
The silhouette can change wildly by host family, but the parasite signature should remain
obvious.

## Threat tiers
- **Swarm** — the cannon-fodder masses you mow down: [[Swarm-Ripper]] and [[Swarm-Spitter]].
- **Elites** — tougher, mutated variants with special attacks; spike the pressure mid-run.
  See [[Render]], [[Rot-Engine]], [[Graft-Breacher]].
- **Breach-bosses** — monstrous expressions of the Scourge that hold a breach, such as
  [[Breach-Boss]], [[Trucebreaker]], and [[Orbital-Breach-Carrier]].
- **Orbital infection** — craft and carrier forms such as [[Scourge-Fighter]].
