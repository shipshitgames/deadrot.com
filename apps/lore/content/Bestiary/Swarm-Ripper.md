# Swarm Ripper

**At a glance:** Scourge melee fodder · rushes the nearest target with blade forearms · dies fast but kills with mass, toxic-green breach cores under black chitin · appears in [[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Deadlane]] / [[Redline]].

Fast melee Scourge fodder that floods lanes and breach rooms through numbers and claws.

## Behaviour
- Rushes the closest target.
- Attacks with blade-like forearms.
- Dies quickly but creates pressure through mass.

## Gameplay Read
- Hunched forward silhouette.
- Clear claws.
- Small toxic-green breach organs under black chitin.

## Visual Motifs
- Black chitin.
- Raw red muscle.
- Wet gore and exposed sinew.
- No human armor layout.
