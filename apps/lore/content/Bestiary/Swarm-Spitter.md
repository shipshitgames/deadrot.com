# Swarm Spitter

**At a glance:** [[Scourge]] ranged form · fires toxic projectiles from throat, chest sac, or fused arm-lance · keeps distance and herds you for the melee swarm · thinner silhouette than [[Swarm-Ripper]] with a toxic-green glow weakpoint · appears in [[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Deadlane]] / [[Rothulk]].

Ranged Scourge form that turns infection into projectile pressure.

## Behaviour
- Keeps distance when possible.
- Fires toxic projectiles from a throat, chest sac, or fused arm-lance.
- Supports melee swarms by forcing movement.

## Gameplay Read
- Thinner than [[Swarm-Ripper]].
- Firing organ must be obvious.
- Toxic-green glow reads as threat source.

## Visual Motifs
- Chitin and raw sinew.
- Distended sac or arm-lance.
- Rusted stolen tech fragments embedded in flesh.
- Wet, parasitic surfaces.

## Sprite Brief
- Views: front, side, back.
- Projectile organ must remain readable in side view.

## Prompt Seed
Scourge ranged parasite, thin predatory silhouette, distended toxic-green firing sac,
bio-industrial arm lance, black chitin, raw sinew, rusted tech fragments, no neon.
