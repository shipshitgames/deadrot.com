# Trucebreaker

**At a glance:** [[Scourge]] neutral objective boss · spawns center and pressures both teams equally · toxic-green core monster big enough to force a human truce · appears in [[Pactfall]].

Neutral Scourge arena threat in [[Pactfall]] that can force Pyre and Wardens to stop fighting each other.

## Behaviour
- Spawns from the neutral center or contested objective.
- Pressures both teams equally.
- A large enough surge can force a temporary human truce.

## Gameplay Read
- Objective boss silhouette.
- Toxic-green core visible from top-down/MOBA camera.
- Attacks should disrupt lanes and punish ignored objectives.

## Visual Motifs

- Chitin, raw tissue, rust, and bone-like protrusions.
- Toxic-green breach organ in the center.
