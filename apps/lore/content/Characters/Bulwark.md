# Bulwark

**At a glance:** [[The-Pyre]] heavy Purger · walking furnace, not a Warden wall-builder · slowest but most durable Pyre avatar, heavy cannon or shotgun · appears in [[Scourge-Survivors]].

Heavy Pyre Purger built to stand in breach pressure longer than anyone should.

## Role
- Allegiance: [[The-Pyre]].
- Function: heavy armor and heavy weapons for [[Scourge-Survivors]].
- Fantasy: walking furnace, not a defensive Warden wall-builder.

## Gameplay Read
- Wide silhouette.
- Heavy cannon or shotgun.
- Slowest but most durable Pyre avatar.

## Visual Motifs
- Broad scorched plates and reinforced boots.
- Furnace vents in chest, shoulders, and forearms.
- Blood-hot red heat scars under blackened armor.
- Bone ceramic panels cracked from repeated breach descents.

## Sprite Brief

- Make shoulders and weapon mass obvious.
- Avoid square Warden engineering language; this should still feel Pyre-aggressive.
