# Pyre Courier

**At a glance:** [[The-Pyre]] Purger courier · trades armor for top speed and aggressive boost · clips [[Redline|repeater-pylons]] that send the local horde feral and blind · appears in [[Redline]].

A Purger who runs a burn order down the dead roads faster than the Choir can close them.

## Role
- Allegiance: [[The-Pyre]].
- Function: high-speed courier for [[Redline]] — carries a Pyre burn order between holdouts when the breaches sever the wired relays.
- Fantasy: the Pyre creed at a sprint — don't hold, don't hide, *outrun it and burn what slows you.*

## Gameplay Read
- Light, forward-leaning silhouette built for pure momentum.
- Trades armor for top speed and aggressive boost.
- Clips [[Redline|repeater-pylons]] on the way past — each one killed sends the local horde feral and blind ([[CANON]] §6).

## Visual Motifs
- Stripped scorched plate and ash-cloth streamers — nothing that isn't moving.
- Blood-hot Pyre heat lines trailing back along the limbs.
- Furnace visor and a sealed message-canister burning hellfire orange.
- Rust-and-gunmetal road grime; no neon.

## Sprite Brief
- Views: front, side (the hero read — a full-tilt run), back.
- Must read as *speed* even static; leaner and faster-looking than [[Vector]].
