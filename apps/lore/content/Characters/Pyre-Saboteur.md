# Pyre Saboteur

**At a glance:** [[The-Pyre]] solo infiltrator / saboteur · light agile climber built for jumps, swings, and wall-work, not a firefight · platforms up the [[The-Rothulk|bio-hulk]] to ignite the [[CANON|breach-core]] while slipping the Choir's massed sight · appears in [[Rothulk]].

The lone Purger who climbs a living breach-ship to its heart and sets the core on fire.

## Role
- Allegiance: [[The-Pyre]].
- Function: solo infiltrator for [[Rothulk]] — platforms up the [[The-Rothulk|bio-hulk]] to ignite the [[CANON|breach-core]] and sever the local node from the Choir.
- Fantasy: the [[Premise|Descent]] made playable — *to hold is to die slowly; the only cure is fire*, carried one body deep into the nest.

## Gameplay Read
- Light, agile climber built for jumps, swings, and wall-work — not a firefight.
- Carries sabotage charges; the run is about *reaching* the core, not clearing the room.
- Survives by staying out of the Choir's massed sight ([[CANON]] §5) — route through the gaps between clusters.

## Visual Motifs
- Stripped scorched infiltration rig, low-profile and dark.
- A furnace visor dimmed to a sliver so the nest can't read the glow.
- An ignition charge / breach-torch burning hellfire orange at the hip.
- Bone-ceramic grip plates, blood-hot heat scars, rust and gore from the climb; no neon.

## Sprite Brief
- Views: front, side (the platforming read — mid-climb / mid-leap), back.
- A lean, vertical mover — clearly lighter than [[Bulwark]], more covert than [[Vector]].
