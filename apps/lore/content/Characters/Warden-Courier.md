# Warden Courier

**At a glance:** [[The-Wardens]] courier · heavier, steadier runner that trades top speed for control and survivability · reinforced cargo rig keeps the hold order intact through hits · appears in [[Redline]].

A Warden who carries a hold order through the rot and intends to deliver it intact.

## Role
- Allegiance: [[The-Wardens]].
- Function: high-speed courier for [[Redline]] — runs a Warden hold order between holdouts when the lanes go dark.
- Fantasy: Warden discipline at a sprint — speed as a duty, the message protected like a wall.

## Gameplay Read
- Heavier, steadier runner than the [[Pyre-Courier]] — trades top speed for control and survivability.
- Reinforced cargo rig; the order survives even when the runner takes hits.
- Holds momentum through contact rather than gambling everything on raw pace.

## Visual Motifs
- Gunmetal road armor with industrial-yellow hazard stripes, used sparingly.
- Reinforced courier harness and a sealed, armored message-case.
- Ember rangefinder lamps; worn steel, weld scars, field repairs.
- Boxy, modular Warden shape language.

## Sprite Brief
- Views: front, side (running), back.
- Reads as a *protected* runner — sturdier and more deliberate than the [[Pyre-Courier]].

## Prompt Seed
Warden human courier running, gunmetal road armor, industrial yellow hazard stripes,
armored message case, reinforced harness, ember rangefinder lamps, worn steel and weld
scars, boxy modular silhouette, DOOM-like blood/rust/gunmetal, no neon.
