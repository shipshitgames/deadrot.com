## Visual Thesis

split open by Scourge parasite tissue, one arm still reading as heavy siege
machinery and the other side collapsing into wet tendril mass.

## Silhouette Lock

- Primary read: slow structure-killer, not infantry.
- Proportion: larger than [[Swarm-Ripper]], smaller and less godlike than [[Breach-Boss]].
- Iconic shape signature: one oversized demolition claw plus exposed parasite back.
- Forbidden shape drift: clean mech, normal zombie, symmetrical knight, generic demon.

## Material And Color Lock

- Body materials: rusted hull plating, pistons, black chitin, raw parasite tissue.
- Accent materials: old warning-stripe paint, cracked industrial panels, bone-sinew claw edges.
- Emissive color: toxic green at former control nodes and breach cores.
- Forbidden colors: clean sci-fi blue, neon body paint, bright chrome.
