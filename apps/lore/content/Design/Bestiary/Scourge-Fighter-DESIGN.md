## Visual Thesis

Small infected attack craft: broken metal hull swallowed by Scourge growth,
clean enough to read at arcade speed but still wet, chitinous, and hostile.

## Silhouette Lock

- Primary read: small craft, not winged beast.
- Proportion: compact wedge or stinger form with an obvious forward direction.
- Iconic shape signature: toxic-green core/eye embedded in rusted hull and chitin.
- Forbidden shape drift: clean fighter jet, fantasy bat, generic insect, smooth drone.

## Material And Color Lock

- Body materials: rusted hull, black chitin, raw tissue in engine gaps.
- Accent materials: living spore pods and broken fins.
- Emissive color: toxic green core/eye only.
- Forbidden colors: clean cockpit blue, neon trim, glossy chrome.
