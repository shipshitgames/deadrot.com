## Visual Thesis

Fast rot-flesh melee fodder: a hunched parasite body that reads first as blade
forearms, exposed muscle, black chitin, and one small toxic breach core.

## Silhouette Lock

- Primary read: low, forward, rushing melee body.
- Proportion: broad shoulders, narrow waist, digitigrade or clawed stance.
- Iconic shape signature: body-grown bone sword forearms.
- Forbidden shape drift: clean armored soldier, elegant demon, smooth alien,
  symmetrical knight, neon cyber creature.

## Material And Color Lock

- Body materials: raw blood-red muscle, black chitin, exposed sinew.
- Accent materials: cracked bone blades and small bone plates.
- Emissive color: toxic green only at breach core/node.
- Forbidden colors: magenta, cyan, blue neon, clean white highlights.
