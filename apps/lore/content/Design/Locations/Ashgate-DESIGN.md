## Visual Thesis

The hard industrial threshold where Pyre breach-descent begins: ash, gate
machinery, scorched metal, and organized violence around a wound in the lanes.

## Silhouette Lock

- Primary read: fortified industrial gate and descent infrastructure.
- Iconic shape signature: angular gate machinery, red-hot breach seams, Pyre
  burn marks.
- Forbidden shape drift: clean sci-fi city, beige fantasy ruin, lush arena.

## Material And Color Lock

- Materials: gunmetal, rust, ash, cracked concrete, blackened barricades.
- Emissive: hellfire and blood-hot industrial glow; toxic only at Scourge breach
  contamination.
- Forbidden colors: blue tech glow, clean white panels, neon city lighting.
