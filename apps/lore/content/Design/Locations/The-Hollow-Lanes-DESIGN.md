## Visual Thesis

Collapsed transit arteries turned into echoing kill corridors: lane geometry,
dead infrastructure, Scourge pressure, and sparse Warden/Pyre salvage.

## Silhouette Lock

- Primary read: long broken corridors and lane forks.
- Iconic shape signature: repeating tunnel ribs, collapsed signage, breach
  growth interrupting human infrastructure.
- Forbidden shape drift: open wilderness, clean subway, bright sci-fi hallway.

## Material And Color Lock

- Materials: cracked asphalt/concrete, rail metal, soot, rust, cable bundles.
- Emissive: scarce hellfire utility light and toxic breach nodes.
- Forbidden colors: saturated cyberpunk signage, clean blue lighting, soft pastel
  ambience.
