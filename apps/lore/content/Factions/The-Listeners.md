# The Listeners

**At a glance:** heresy story-thread, NOT a fourth playable faction · reject the [[The-Pyre|Pyre]]'s fire and the [[The-Wardens|Wardens]]' walls · study and steer the [[Scourge|Choir]] instead of killing it · heretics to both, protected by neither · drives narrative/missions in [[War-for-the-Lanes]].

*The third answer nobody wanted.* A fringe heresy that rejects both the [[The-Pyre|Pyre]]'s
fire and the [[The-Wardens|Wardens]]' walls. The Listeners believe the **Choir** ([[CANON]]
§5) is not a thing to be destroyed but a thing to be **understood** — and, if it can be
understood, perhaps *steered, starved of purpose, or spoken to.* The Scourge is not evil; it
only optimizes to survive ([[CANON]] §1). The Listeners ask the forbidden question: *what
happens if we change what it's optimizing for?*

## The heresy
To study the Choir you must get close to it — tap its signal, keep severed nodes alive to
observe, sometimes **let it in** under control. To the Pyre this is consorting with the
extinction of the species; to the Wardens it's a breach in the line waiting to happen. Both
hunt or exile Listeners. The Listeners answer that fire and walls have *never once* killed the
Scourge — they only buy time — and that the only path that *ends* it runs through its mind.

## Why it fits — the AI parable
This is the universe's alignment debate made flesh. Three responses to an unaligned
optimizing intelligence: **shut it down** (Pyre), **box it / contain it** (Wardens),
**understand and steer it** (the Listeners). Each is a coherent, defensible, dangerous bet.

## Stance
- Vs [[Scourge|the Scourge]]: study and steer, not destroy.
- Vs other factions: heretics to both [[The-Pyre]] and [[The-Wardens]]; protected by neither.
  They keep to the **Pact** in the field but are tolerated, not trusted.

## Status
**Canon as a story thread** (see [[CANON]] §8). Not a fourth playable faction — the
[[War-for-the-Lanes]] frame stays three-sided (Pyre / Wardens / Scourge). The Listeners drive
narrative and missions. *Open: whether they ever graduate to a full faction.*
