# The Pyre

**At a glance:** faction · zealot order broken from the [[The-Wardens|Wardens]] after [[Zero-Day]] · doctrine of starve-and-sever — burn the hosts, gut the Choir repeaters from inside · fast, expendable, high-risk DPS/infiltration · appears in [[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Pactfall]].

Zealot operators who descend *into* breaches to **cauterize the Scourge at its source**.
Fast, expendable, scorched-earth. They believe the only cure is fire.

## Who they are
A **zealot order that broke away from the [[The-Wardens|Wardens]]** in the years after
[[Zero-Day]]. They watched humanity "hold the line" and saw only a slower extinction — so
they made a creed of the one thing the Choir fears: getting *close enough to burn the source*.
Faith-driven, expendable by choice, organized in cells rather than chains of command. A
Purger's life is spent, gladly, to sever one more node. Where the Wardens want to *outlast*
the Scourge, the Pyre want to *make it pay for every world.*

Against the Scourge's weakness ([[CANON]] §6) their doctrine is **starve and sever**: burn
the hosts it feeds on and destroy the **Choir repeaters** from the inside, so the local horde
goes feral.

## Playstyle
Aggressive, high-risk DPS / infiltration. → [[Scourge-Survivors]] (FPS).

## Rivalry (not war)
Call [[The-Wardens]] cowards who prolong the rot by merely holding lines — but fight beside
them the instant a breach opens (see [[Premise]] §The Pact). The feud is settled in the
arena, not the field: [[Pactfall]].
