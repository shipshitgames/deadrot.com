# The Wardens

**At a glance:** faction — remnant military/engineering corps · holds the lanes with towers, walls, and attrition · fragment-and-isolate doctrine, slow to move and impossible to rout · appears in [[Deadlane]] / [[Pactfall]].

Engineers and gunners who **hold the lanes** against the Scourge with towers, walls, and
attrition. Methodical, patient — survival over glory.

## Who they are
The **remnant military and engineering corps** — what survived of the old-world armies after
[[Zero-Day]]. They kept the chain of command, the hardware, and the doctrine, and reorganized
into the closest thing humanity still has to a **state**: hierarchical, disciplined, slow to
move and impossible to rout. They believe the species survives by *outlasting* the Scourge,
not charging it. To the Wardens, every wall held is a generation bought.

Against the Scourge's weakness ([[CANON]] §6) their doctrine is **fragment and isolate**:
hold the chokepoints to break the swarm's density and cut it off from **the Choir**, so what
gets through arrives feral.

## Playstyle
Defensive / economic / building. → [[Deadlane]] (tower defense).

## Rivalry (not war)
See [[The-Pyre]] as reckless fanatics who waste lives on suicide runs into breaches that
just reopen — yet hold the line *for* them every time the Scourge surges (see [[Premise]]
§The Pact). The feud is settled in the arena: [[Pactfall]].
