# Brawl

**At a glance:** playable prototype / one-on-one battlefield fighter · pick a
Pact or Scourge fighter, let the front assign the rival, and settle a single
arcade round · a sprite-learning game for fighting stances, hit reads, guards,
hurt states, and KO animation · feeds the [[Warline]] idea as a contested-front
duel.

Brawl is the character-scale pressure valve for the [[War-for-the-Lanes]]. Where
[[Pactfall]] turns the human rivalry into a lane objective, Brawl turns a
battlefield incident into one close fight: two champions, one strip of ground,
and a result the front can understand.

## Canon Role

Brawl outcomes are **prototype war signals**, not permanent canon by default. A
duel can rally a lane, humiliate a faction, or mark a Scourge champion for later
lore, but authored canon still has to promote the event.

## First Roster Target

- Pyre: [[Pyre-Duelist]], [[Pyre-Cauterizer]].
- Wardens: [[Warden-Bastion]], [[Warden-Artillerist]].
- Scourge: [[Render]], [[Trucebreaker]].

## Sprite Target

The production target is a real fighting-game sheet per character: idle, walk,
jump, crouch, guard, light, heavy, special, hurt, knockdown, KO, win, and taunt.
The current prototype can reuse existing package entity plates while the rules
and camera prove the pipeline.

## Status

Playable prototype — first cut proves character select, game-selected rival,
movement, guard, light/heavy/special attacks, KO, rematch, and Warline record
banking.
