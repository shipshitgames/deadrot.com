# Rothulk

**At a glance:** infiltration platformer (Mario-like) · climb a living [[Scourge]] bio-hulk to its breach-core, ignite it, sever the local node · lone [[The-Pyre|Pyre]] saboteur · the [[CANON]] §6 win condition as a level — node isolation from the inside, the Pyre creed made playable.

*Burn it from the inside.* A lone [[The-Pyre|Pyre]] saboteur platforms up through a living
[[Scourge]] **bio-hulk** — a crashed [[Cosmology|breach-ship]] grown into the ground — to
reach its **breach-core**, ignite it, and **sever the local node** from [[CANON|the Choir]].
"Rothulk" is the Pyre's name for one of these beached breach-ships: a mountain of rotting
host-flesh and dead-world machinery with a heartbeat.

## Canon role
The infiltration front, and the [[CANON]] §6 win condition rendered as a level: get *inside*,
kill the **repeaters**, and the surrounding horde goes feral — blind, dumb, instinct-only.
Where [[Scourge-Survivors]] is a frantic gunfight room-to-room and [[Deadlane]] holds the
lanes from outside, Rothulk is the **quiet climb up the spine of the nest** to the core that
keeps it connected. Tonally the Pyre creed made playable: *to hold is to die slowly; the only
cure is fire.* (See [[Premise]], [[Timeline|The Descent]].)

## The hook — platform up a living nest
The bio-hulk is the level *and* the enemy. You ascend chambers of pulsing host-flesh, ride
spore-geysers, ride and crush living platforms, and route around the Choir's sight by staying
**outside its massed clusters** — because while the breach-core is lit and connected, the
whole hulk knows where you are ([[CANON]] §5). Reach the core, **ignite it**, then platform
back out ahead of the collapse as the severed node turns feral around you.

## Core Loop
Tight, readable single-saboteur platforming on the shared [[Survivors-Loop]] frame:
infiltrate → climb the bio-hulk's chambers chaining jumps, swings, and crushed enemies →
collect salvage/XP → draft movement and sabotage-charge upgrades → reach and ignite the
**breach-core** → escape the collapsing, now-severed hulk (or get caught in the spawn). Each
hulk is a self-contained nest-vertical; lighting the core **fragments the Choir locally** and
hands the cleared lane back to the war.

## Enemy shape
A breach turned into a tower: walls of grafted host-flesh, [[Graft-Breacher]] growths that
seal your route, spore-traps, and the **breach-core** itself — the repeater-heart that has to
be reached and burned. Mass-clusters glow with Choir-coordination; the gaps between them are
your path.
