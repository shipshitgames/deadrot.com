# Timeline

> Anchor events of the shared universe. Order is locked; exact dates are [[CANON|open]].

- **Zero Day — the Invasion.** First contact with [[Scourge|the Scourge]] from the void.
  Humanity fights the orbital and sky battle **and loses** — not ready, too many. The world
  falls; the first breaches root groundside. The origin game: [[Zero-Day]]. *(See [[CANON]] §3.)*
- **The Long Fall.** The months after Zero Day: cities consumed, fleets gone, survivors
  scattering down the only roads left — the **lanes**. The old world ends here.
- **The Schism.** The survivors split by doctrine. The remnant army holds and rebuilds as the
  [[The-Wardens|Wardens]]; a zealot order breaks away — the [[The-Pyre|Pyre]] — convinced that
  holding is just a slower death and the only cure is fire.
- **The Pact.** After the rivalry nearly costs a holdout its walls, both sides swear the
  **Pact**: whatever the feud, humans stand together the instant a breach opens. The feud is
  exiled to sanctioned arenas ([[Pactfall]]), never the field.
- **The Lane Wars.** The Resistance fortifies the lanes and learns the Scourge's secret —
  that cutting its **repeaters** severs it from the [[CANON|Choir]]. War becomes a fight to
  **fragment the signal**. ([[Deadlane]] era.) When the Scourge cuts the lanes and comms
  between holdouts, lone runners carry the message on foot, faster than the horde can close
  ([[Redline]]).
- **The Descent.** The first Purgers go *into* the breaches to sever nodes and burn sources
  from the inside — most do not come back. ([[Scourge-Survivors]] / [[Perdition]] era.) Pyre
  saboteurs climb the living breach-ships to ignite their cores and sever the local node
  ([[Rothulk]]).
- **The Listeners emerge.** A heresy spreads: that the Choir could be *understood and steered*
  rather than only fought. Hunted by Pyre and Wardens alike, the [[The-Listeners]] keep
  severed nodes alive and listen. *(Concept — see their page.)*
