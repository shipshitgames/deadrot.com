# War for the Lanes — the persistent layer (north star)

The long-term vision: a **persistent shared world** where the three factions
([[The-Pyre]], [[The-Wardens]], and [[Scourge|the Scourge]]) fight over a live map of lanes
and breaches — and where playing the small games *shifts the front*.

## Path (realistic, vibe-codeable)
1. Ship the small games ([[Scourge-Survivors]], [[Deadlane]], [[Pactfall]], [[Starblight]])
   as proofs + audience builders.
2. Add a persistent **War for the Lanes** metagame: a shared map (PartyKit/edge + a DB)
   where players' results move a live front between faction control. MMO-lite, achievable.
3. A **Dofus-like server** — persistent, instanced, isometric/tactical (see the
   `isometric-3d` genre) — is the far horizon once the metagame proves out.

## Why it fits
Three asymmetric factions warring over territory is a textbook MMO/RTS premise. Designing
units and lore for 3 factions now keeps this door open at zero cost.
