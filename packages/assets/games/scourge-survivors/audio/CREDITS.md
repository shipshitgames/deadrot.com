Notes:
- Weapon SFX are transcoded **→ WebM/Opus** (the studio audio format), cover art stripped. Masters not kept in-repo.
- **SFX play as authored samples; the procedural synth stays the fallback** for any cue without a file or if a sample fails to decode. Pistol, sniper, SMG, shotgun, and cannon now have authored sample files; reload/hit/explosion/other one-offs stay procedural.
- **Music license (Suno) warning:** the studio's `game-asset-pipeline` says avoid Udio/Suno for shipped in-game loops (license uncertainty) — these are **placeholders**; prefer Soundraw / Beatoven before public release.
- **SFX license (ElevenLabs) warning:** paid plans grant commercial use, but generated SFX **may not be resold/distributed as standalone files or sample packs** — they must stay embedded in the game (fine here). SFX may be sublicensed to other ElevenLabs users by default — flip the **"Disable"** toggle on the Sound Effects page. Confirm the generating account's plan covers commercial use.
